About us

Passionate creators of delicious, high-quality food and beverages

Our story

In 2015, YummyBox was born out of a simple love for indulgence—decadent desserts, creamy dairy delights, freshly baked pastries, and refreshing beverages, all made the way they should be: with real ingredients and a whole lot of passion.

What started as a tiny bakery experiment quickly grew into a beloved brand, where every treat is crafted with care. We believe in the magic of hand-whipped creams, slow-churned custards, buttery flaky layers, and drinks that taste like happiness in a glass.

Today, we still make everything in small batches, because quality should never be rushed.From our kitchen to your table, every bite and sip is a little reminder that life is sweeter when it's made with love.

Our mission

At YummyBox, we believe that extraordinary desserts do more than satisfy cravings
-they create moments of joy, connection, and celebration.
Every treat we craft is made with a purpose: to delight your senses, honor tradition, and care for the planet.

Exceptional Ingredients

We partner with trusted suppliers who share our dedication to quality, freshness, and ethical sourcing. Every bite reflects the love we put into the best.

Artisan Craftsmanship

Honoring pastry traditions while embracing innovation, we craft desserts where classic techniques meet modern creativity—resulting in unforgettable flavors and textures

Baked with Responsibility

From kitchen to packaging, we minimize waste and choose eco-friendly practices—because delicious treats should never come at the Earth's expense.
